# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

# DO'S
- Think before posting – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.

- Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal information like phone numbers or addresses.

- Verify information before sharing – Check if the news or content is from a reliable source.

- Be respectful and positive – Engage in healthy discussions and avoid offensive language.

- Use social media productively – Follow educational pages, join academic groups, and build your professional network.

# DONT'S

- Don’t overshare personal details – Avoid posting sensitive or private information.

- Don’t engage in cyberbullying – Never post hurtful comments or troll others.

- Don’t spread misinformation – Avoid sharing unverified or fake news.

- Don’t waste excessive time – Limit screen time to maintain productivity.

- Don’t post inappropriate content – Avoid content that can harm your reputation or future career.
