# "Stay Safe Online" Checklist for College Students in India
 # General Safety
 Use strong, unique passwords for each account (mix of letters, numbers, symbols).
 Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps like email, banking, and social media.
 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities (like logging into banking apps).
 Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.
 Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown attachments from emails/WhatsApp.
 Limit personal information shared on social media (phone number, address, college ID, etc.).
  # UPI & Financial Safety
  Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank.
  Always verify payment requests—receiving money does NOT require entering a UPI PIN.
  Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money via apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
  Avoid scanning unknown QR codes, especially from strangers or social media posts.
